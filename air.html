What is Air Quality

“Air quality” refers to the condition of the surrounding air in the atmosphere. Good air quality refers to the degree which the air is clean, clear and free from pollutants such as smoke, dust and smog as well as other gaseous impurities in the air. Air quality is determined by assessing a variety of pollution indicators. Good air quality is a requirement for preserving the exquisite balance of life on earth for humans, plants, animals and natural resources. Below is an image of the Air Quality Index:
